Physicist, Data Scientist.

I am currently expanding my capabilities as a physicist and data scientist through the Master's program in Physics of Data at the University of Padova. My core expertise lies in translating complex systems into robust, statistically-sound models, moving beyond simple data fitting to build predictive architectures grounded in physical principles. I focus on bridging the gap between fundamental physics and machine learning to extract deep, actionable insights from challenging datasets in a quest to understand and simulate the world around us.
